The Mission
Why this exists

Get measurably stronger, not just play more games. The plan is honest and evidence-led: below ~1300, games are decided by one-move blunders, not opening theory — so we plug the leaks first (tactics & blunder-checking), then layer on opening principles and middlegame plans. Each lesson is a single sitting and ends with a tangible win.
